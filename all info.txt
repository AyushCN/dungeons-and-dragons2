STATUS EFFECTS (CURRENT SYSTEM)

GOOD STATUS (max 1)

REGEN
Heals HP at the start of each round.
Flat healing. No scaling.
Resolves before any damage-over-time effects.
Removed at end of round when duration ends.

BLOCK
Reduces incoming attack damage after a hit.
Does not affect hit chance.
Does not reduce DOT damage.
Removed at end of round when duration ends.

VEIL
Prevents new debuffs from being applied.
Turn 1: debuff blocked 100%.
Turn 2: 65% chance to block.
Turn 3: 40% chance to block.
If a debuff succeeds, Veil is removed at end of round.
Max duration: 3 turns.

FOCUS
Attacks roll two d20 and take the higher.
If either die is a natural 20, it is a critical hit.
Does not increase damage directly.
Removed at end of round when duration ends.

BAD STATUS (max 1)

POISON
Deals damage at the start of each round.
Damage scales exponentially each turn.
Resolves after Regen.
Affected by poison resistance.
Removed at end of round when duration ends.

BURN
Deals flat damage at the start of each round.
Reduces outgoing attack damage by 30%.
No scaling.
Removed at end of round when duration ends.

FREEZE
Deals flat damage at the start of each round.
Forces the target to act last in the round based on chance:
Turn 1: guaranteed last.
Turn 2: 80% chance last.
Turn 3: 70% chance last.
Removed at end of round when duration ends.

STUN
Prevents the target from acting on their turn.
Turn 1: guaranteed skip.
Turn 2: 30% chance to act.
Turn 3: 40% chance to act.
Turn 4: 50% chance to act.
Chance increases by 10% each turn until recovery.
Removed immediately on successful recovery.

GLOBAL RULES (still plain text)

Only one good and one bad status allowed at a time.
Statuses do not replace each other.
If a slot is occupied, new status application fails.
Statuses trigger before turns begin.
Statuses are removed only at end of round unless specified otherwise.
