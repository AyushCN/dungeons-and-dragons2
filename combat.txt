Rule: Attack Roll
An attack roll is 1d20 + attack modifier.
If result >= target Armor Class (AC), the attack hits.

Rule: Critical Hit
A critical hit occurs when a natural 20 is rolled on a d20 attack roll.
When a critical hit occurs, all damage dice are doubled.
Critical hits deal significantly more damage than normal hits.


Rule: Damage Calculation
Damage equals weapon damage dice plus strength modifier.

Rule: Miss
If attack roll is lower than Armor Class, the attack misses and deals no damage.
